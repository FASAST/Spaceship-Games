Star Trek Campaigns - Links

This links page provides a few sources of Star Trek information. Most are really not needed in the campaigns, but I wanted to make the STSSTC (first link below) available to everyone as a reference source, and added the others to fill the page. If anyone has a Star Trek related web site they would like added, just let the campaign master know.

Sorry for the lack of links. There used to be a lot more, but the sites disappeared over the years. [Don't you just love the stability of the Internet?]

Starship Schematic Database Lots of interesting diagrams and graphics, but little of the information included is of any relevance to the campaigns.
Vessels of Starfleet An interesting site, but most of the information is relative to the Second Generation period.
The UFP - StarFleet A collection of pictures of Star Fleet models. This has no use whatsoever in the campaigns. However, it is interesting, and Kent should love it. Also check Griffwork's Shipyard, a related collection of model pictures.
