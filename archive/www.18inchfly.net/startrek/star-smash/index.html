Star Smash

A Star Trek Tournament
using the Starship Tactical Combat Simulator

Star Smash is a round-robin tournament using FASA's Star Trek Starship Tactical Combat Simulator.

This site supports the tournament by providing a place to access general information and allows the players to keep track of what is going on.
