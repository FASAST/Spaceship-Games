Scenario 22 - The Incident at Omicron III

Setup

Scenario 22, prepared by David Ternes

The planet Omicron III occupies the center of the battle area. The planet has one moon located three hexes from the planet, and on a line perpendicular to a line through the starting locations of the two fleets and the planet.

Each fleet starts with its most advanced ship twenty hexes from the edge of the planet The fleets are on opposite sides of the planet. All ships in a force must within five hexes of each other.
