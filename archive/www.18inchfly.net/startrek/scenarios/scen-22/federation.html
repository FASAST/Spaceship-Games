Scenario 22 - The Incident at Omicron III

the United Federation of Planets

Scenario 22, prepared by David Ternes

The United Federation of Planets has recently granted Protectorate status to the Omicron system. The natives of Omicron III are pre-industrial, and have been observed by research and study teams, but not contacted directly. Noting the strategic significance of the system, and the inability of the inhabitants to defend themselves, the UFP wants to keep them safe.

Intelligence has reported that the Klingons are also interested in Omicron as a base for operations within the UFP. They have dispatched a force to begin the conquest of the system. This cannot be allowed to happen.

Commodore Randolph "Randy" Johnston has been ordered to assemble a force from available ships to stop the Klingon attack on Omicron.

Soon after Johnston's squadron arrived at Omicron a group of four Klingon ships was detected approaching the system. Commodore Johnston warned the Klingons that the system was now a protectorate of the UFP. The Klingon answer, freely translated, was "We saw it first! Get out of our way!"

It is now late in the day of star date 1/9108.05. Provisional Squadron Johnston is near Omicron III. The Klingons are approaching, and show no signs of stopping. Lacking any other alternative, Commodore Johnston orders the attack.

Objective. Prevent Klingon troops from landing on Omicron III.

Force. The Star Fleet force consists of the following.

    Name Class Name Type Class CE
    Provisional Squadron Johnston
    Bismark (NCC-1704) Constitution Mk Ia Cruiser XI 62 Flagship
    Salamis (NCC-4341) Larson Mk I Destroyer VI 72
    Napoleon (NCC-4344) Larson Mk I Destroyer VI 67
    Drake (NCC-24240) Nelson Mk I Scout VII 61

Rules of Engagement. The UFP is not at war with the Klingon Empire, and doesn't want to start one over this "incident". Do not destroy their ships. Beat them into helplessness; leave all but one drifting in space; but do not destroy their ships -- unless there is absolutely no other way to stop their invasion or protect your own ships. The high command is counting on Commodore Johnston to fight a battle, but keep the peace.
