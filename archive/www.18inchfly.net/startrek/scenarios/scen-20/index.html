Scenario 20

10 Training Scenarios

Scenario 20, V1.1, 13 October 2001, prepared by David Ternes

This is a group of ten scenarios intended to train new players in the mechanics of the game. There are no fancy descriptions or stories with them. They are intended to be played in open space with each side starting just outside of maximum lock-on range. The scenarios are not arranged in any particular order (well, actually they are in the order in which they were created).

Assign all ships a Crew Efficiency of 70.

Scenarios marked with an asterisk (*) have been play tested.
