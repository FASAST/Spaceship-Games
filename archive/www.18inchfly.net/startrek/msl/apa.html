Rules Notes:

    Assault Ships and Troop Transports are used for carrying marines. Marines to be used to capture a system must come either from these types of ships or from the marines on warships. These ships may also be used to carry spare crew members.

    Colonial Transports are used for the bulk transport of personnel. They may be use to carry spare crew for warships or extra marines. However, marines on this type of ship may not be used to capture a system.
