Star Trek Campaigns - Illegal Ships

Ship designs listed on this page have been found to be "illegal and unusable" under the ship construction rules. This means that the components of the designs were so far off of the ship construction rules that is was not possible to obtain a Combat Efficiency value. While such a situation is traditional (and even expected) in game systems designed by FASA, it is not acceptable in these campaigns. Therefore, these ships will not be allowed in any campaigns.
