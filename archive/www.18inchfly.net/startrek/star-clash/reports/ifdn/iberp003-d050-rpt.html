Patrol Group 3
2/2505.11 • The group patrols the Kinnara-Tannine-Flitner area.
The patrol sector is enlarged to include Colil. The Group is ordered to scout the Colil system and report what it finds.
• The two Federation contacts ignore the patrol. The warpshuttle Mel closes and identifies them as two Lenthal class destroyers. The Lenthals also ignore the close contact. When followed and then observed from the edge of the patrol area, they appear to cover a patrol area of Currie-Flitner-Tannine. It takes 7 days of following and watching to determine this.
2/2505.19 • The patrol approaches Colil. It detects one large and eight medium sized Federation warp signatures in the system. It reports this to IKS fleet headquarters and returns to its patrol duties.
 
Raider Group 4
2/2505.11 • The group is watching the Federation and Klingon forces in the New Paradise system.
Suddenly, two large units of the Federation force charge at the Raider Group. The Group turns and warps out of the system to the "south".
2/2505.12 • Instead of heading back to New Paradise, the Group continues towards Landis Wavamm at warp 8 to arrive on 2/2505.13.
2/2505.13 • The group arrives at Landis Wavamm. Three medium Klingon warp signatures are detected. The group moves in to attack. The enemy stands by the planet. However, the squadron commander (Captain Ribpanno) begins negotiations with the Klingon squadron commander (Captain Roloc). Roloc agrees to allow Ribpanno to plunder the planet as a punishment for its rebellion. Ribpanno is informed that a Klingon assault force is on its way, and things will change when they arrive. Almost gleefully, Ribpanno's marines beam down to the planet and begin stealing anything which looks valuable.
2/2505.16 • Four medium Klingon warp signatures are detected approaching the Landis Wavamm system. The Group orders its troops back to their ships. Loaded with booty (precious metals, valuable minerals, advanced electronic gear, pink feather boas, etc.) they leave the system headed "north" at warp 8 to arrive at New Paradise on 2/2505.18.
2/2505.18 • The force returns to the New Paradise system. It observes the Federation and Klingon forces still in place there.

2/2505.14 • The Group closes on the Comstock system. Two small Federation ships are detected. The group returns to its regular patrol.
