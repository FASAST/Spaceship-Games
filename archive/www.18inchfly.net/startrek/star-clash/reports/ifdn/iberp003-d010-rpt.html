Star Clash - Imperial Klingon States Report
Stardates 2/2504.01 - 2/2504.10 (Days 1-10)

Common
2/2504.01 • All IKS forces remain in their systems.
2/2504.04 • A listening post on Mattara detects messages from the Gamon system indicating that a sizeable Federation force has arrived at the system.
2/2504.05 • The Mattara post senses the passage of a number of ships moving on a course from the area of the Gamon system headed towards the Meadow, Satterfield, or Currie systems.
2/2504.06 • The force is picked up by the North Pasedena and K'Linnsan systems moving "south" well out from the IKS border. Spies in the Satterfield system send a report of a great ball held to celebrate the temporary presence of a Federation force consisting of (according to the news nets); Defense (battle cruiser), Frederick (destroyer), Breitenfeld (destroyer), Marlborough (destroyer), Wellington (destroyer), Bader (scout), and Clifton (scout).
A second Federation force of undetermined size is also detected heading "south" along the IKS border. It does not appear to be stopping at any systems along the way. It disappears from the sensors by late 2/2504.07.
2/2504.07 • The Kinnara system detects a Federation force moving away from the Currie system to the "southeast".
