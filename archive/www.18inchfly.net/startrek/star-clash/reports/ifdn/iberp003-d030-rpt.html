Star Clash - Imperial Klingon States Report
Stardates 2/2504.21 - 2/2504.30 (Days 21-30)

Battle Group 1
2/2504.22 • The IKS government is concerned that Star Fleet leaders in the area, notably Rear Admiral T'nek, have indicated that the IKS should be attacked and eliminated. Because of this, the Battle Group is designated as the center of the new IKS defense plans. Most of the ships in Raider Group 2 and Raider Group 3 are ordered to join Battle Group 1 at Kinarra.
• The Battle Group leaves K'linsann for Kinarra. At warp 6 it arrives the next day.
• Kapak's Scout Squadron is detached and forms Patrol Group 5.
2/2504.23 • Ships from Raider Group 2 and Raider Group 3 arrive and join Battle Group 1.
2/2504.24 • Neumar's Support Squadron is detached from Battle Group 1 and assigned to the garrison at Kinarra. This allows the group to have a cruising speed of warp 7.
 
Raider Group 2
2/2504.21 • The Nukrit (scout) and the Vut (warpshuttle) are detached and form Patrol Group 6.
• All ships in the group, except M'lunder's Squadron, are ordered to Kinarra.
2/2504.22 • The group is ordered to the Farkin system. A Federation fleet is reported to be there assaulting the system. The group will shadow that fleet, report on its movements, and take advantage of any opportunity to capture ships after a battle. The group will arrive on 2/2505.01.
2/2504.23 • The ships detached from the group arrive at Kinarra. They join Battle Group 1.
 
Patrol Group 3
2/2504.21 • The unit is renamed as "Patrol Group 3".
• All ships in the group, except the Xekrid (scout) and the Mel (warpshuttle) are ordered to Kinarra. The group is ordered to patrol the Kinnara-Tannine-Flitner area. On the approach of significant enemy forces they are to issue a warning and follow them into the IKS.
2/2504.23 • The ships detached from the group arrive at Kinarra. They join Battle Group 1.
 
Raider Group 4
2/2504.21 • The group continues to sit at Deerlam watching the Star Fleet force performing maneuvers.
2/2504.29 • The Star Fleet force leaves Deerlam headed "southwest". The Raider Group follows.
 
Patrol Group 5
2/2504.21 • The group is formed at Kinarra and includes Kapak's Scout Squadron (two K-17D Death Stalker class scouts) from Battle Group 1. It is ordered to patrol the Currie-Satterfeld-Meadow area. On the approach of significant enemy forces they are to issue a warning and follow them into the IKS.
 
Patrol Group 6
2/2504.21 • The group is formed at Penchan and includes the Nukrit (a K-17D Death Stalker class scout) and the Vut (a W-2A Koreba class warpshuttle) from Raider Group 2. It is ordered to patrol the Mattarra-Newlin-Gamon area. On the approach of significant enemy forces they are to issue a warning and follow them into the IKS.

2/2504.27 • Intelligence reports that decrypted messages indicate a Star Fleet force is assaulting the Farkin system.
