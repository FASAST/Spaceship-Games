Raider Group 4
2/2505.01 • The group continues to follow the Federation force.
The Star Fleet unit arrives at the Altharra system. Two medium and two small Federation signatures are detected in the system near the inhabited planet.
As the group enters the outer edges of the system two large Federation ships turn quickly and race toward the shadowing vessels. The raider group turns and runs away at warp 8. Although faster, the pursuers, being at warp speed, are unable to engage. They do close sufficiently to be identified as two Alaska class battle cruisers. After a short period paralleling the group's course, the Alaskas turn back and rejoin their fleet.
2/2505.02 • The raider group spends most of a day making sure they are not in immediate danger. It then heads back to Altharra. The Federation force is still there.
2/2505.03 • Two medium sized ships detach from the Federation fleet and head to the "west" at warp 6. They soon disappear from the group's sensors.
2/2505.05 • The Federation force, now consisting of eight large, four medium, and two small ships leaves the Altharra system headed "southwest" at warp 6. The shadows follow.
2/2505.08 • The enemy enters the New Paradise system. Four small Klingon ships withdraw to the "southwest" at warp 5. The Federation fleet moves to the planet.
2/2505.09 • The Star Fleet ships begin landing troops on the planet.
• Four small Klingon warp signatures approach the system from the "southwest". They take up a position well away from both the Star Fleet force and the raider group.

Common
2/2505.02 • The communications channels are crowded with messages from Mantiev. On 2/2505.01 two Klingon battle groups entered the Mantiev system. They attacked and destroyed the Mantiev's Mobile Defense Force and the garrison in the Mantiev system. After the battle, the Klingons boarded the repair outpost in orbit around the planet. They took the outpost in tow and left the system.
2/2505.03 • Mantiev reports that the Klingons have left their system. The Klingon forces appeared to split up.
A group of ten medium Klingon ships headed "northwest".
A group of three large, six medium, and three small Klingon ships headed "north".
A group of three large, eighteen medium, and four small Klingon ships headed "south".
A group of two medium Klingon ships headed "west". This is the group with the captured repair facility.
2/2505.05 • The Landis Wavamm system comes on the communications waves reporting that it has thrown off the Klingon garrison. When the garrison's squadron of star ships left the system, the inhabitants were able to defeat the Klingon forces. They urgently request supplies of small arms and warships to allow themselves to keep their independence.
2/2505.06 • The Altharra system makes it commonly known that a Federation fleet under the command of Rear Admiral T'nek was at that system from 2/2505.02 through 2/2505.05. It included three large and twenty six medium sized ships, one of which was the cruiser Lifeforce. The force left Altharra headed "southwest" at warp 6 followed by three Klingon ships.
2/2505.08 • The Landis Wavamm system reports that the garrison ships have returned. They are Roloc's Escort Squadron of three K-24A Winner class escorts. Will someone please come and drive them off?
