Star Clash - United Federation of Planets Report
Stardates 2/2505.01 - 2/2505.10 (Days 31-40)

General Communications
2/2505.01 • From: Star Fleet Central Command
To: Commander Star Base 10
Info: Commander Triangle Sector
   Upon arrival of force from Outpost 1 at Star Base 10, transfer all excess Star Fleet personnel to the assault ship Asia. Asia will receive separate orders as to the destinations of these personnel.
2/2505.02 • From: Commander Triangle Sector
To: Star Fleet Central Command
   Urgently and respectfully request a modification of the orders for the assault ship Asia. Admiral Kinnison requests that the Asia with her embarked personnel be sent to Baker's World to deliver 644 crewmen for the ships in TF24 that are currently in need of replacements. In addition to the crewmen, the Asia will also need to carry 25 Marines for assignment to the Tuscaloosa (NCC-5042). Admiral Kinnison believes it is of greater urgency to the Federation efforts in the Triangle to have TF24 brought up to full complement as quickly as possible. As soon as repairs are completed and crew reassigned, TF24 will rejoin the fight to push the upstart Klingons out of The Triangle and back to their own space.
2/2505.03 • From: Star Fleet Central Command
To: Commander Triangle Sector
   The orders for Asia are rescinded and it is returned to Triangle Sector control. As replacement personnel were already available at Star Base 10, and the fleet has been in need of replacements for over a week, Star Fleet Headquarters must wonder why the personnel were not sent out on Asia earlier.
   If there are any excess personnel from Outpost 1 left after assignments to Task Force 24 are complete, the Asia is to detach from Triangle Sector control and execute its previous orders.
2/2505.10 • From: Star Fleet Central Command
To: Commander Triangle Sector
   On 2/2504.29 this headquarters ordered Vice Admiral Kinneson to "Send a copy of your plans for the war to us immediately for review." As of this date no plans have been received. Commander Triangle Sector is again ordered to submit complete campaign plans or face immediate relief for cause.

Task Force 89
2/2505.01 • Task Force 89 arrives at the Altharra system. As they enter the outer edges of the system the battle cruisers Resolution and Defence turn quickly and race toward the shadowing vessels. The shadows turn and race away at warp 8. Although faster, the battle cruisers, being at warp speed, are unable to engage. They do close sufficiently to identify the vessels as three D-7 cruisers. After a short period paralleling the Klingons' course, the pursuers turn back and rejoin the fleet.
While the Alaskas are busy, the rest of the fleet enters the system. Two medium and two small Federation signatures are detected. The system's governor contacts Rear Admiral T'nek. He is polite, but makes its clear he is not happy to see a large fleet in his system.
Admiral T'nek exercises that skill so few Vulcans have (lying through their teeth);
"Our apologies, but intelligence reports made it appear that the Klingon Empire was considering an assault on your sovereign territory, so we moved into a position where we could be of assistance. This has proven to be false, as the Klingons instead appear to have attacked a different target. We will leave your domain for as long as your independence remains intact."
2/2505.02 • The planetary governor again contacts Admiral T'nek, and asks how long he intends to remain in the system. He asks if Mantiev is not the location of the Klingon attack T'nek expected on Altharra. While he appreciates the Federation's attention, shouldn't the fleet be headed for Mantiev?
• The shadowing ships return to the system and take up a position on the other side of the sun from the task force.
2/2505.03 • The 349th Scout Division (Bader and Clifton) is detached from the fleet and ordered to patrol the Takanarra-Epliey-Altharra area until given further orders. They are to engage any Klingon force no larger than 2 ships.
• The planetary governor again contacts Admiral T'nek, and asks how long he intends to remain in the system. He is not pleased with the imprecise nature of the answer.
2/2505.04 • The planetary governor again contacts Admiral T'nek, he is very obviously not happy at not knowing when the fleet will leave, but notes that there is nothing he can do about it.
2/2505.05 • The task force departs the Altharra system at warp 6 headed for New Paradise. They expect to arrive on 2/2505.08.
The shadow force also follows.
• No one from the planet makes any contact with the fleet as it departs. Two medium sized ships follow it until it is about a half day away. They then turn back.
2/2505.08 • The task force arrives at the New Paradise system. As they approach they detect four small Klingon warp signatures. The Klingon ships leave the system headed "southwest" at warp 5.
The shadow force takes up a position on the other side of the system..
2/2505.09 • The task force begins landing troops. A total of 400 marines, 250 from the Badger and 150 from the Bache, are placed on the planet.
• Four small Klingon ships approach the system from the "southwest". They take up a position well away from the task force and sit quietly.

Common
2/2505.01 • The AOFW Space Navy reports that a force of three ships with Klingon warp signatures passed through their space. They entered near the Hoot system and left near Martin's Star.
2/2505.02 • The communications channels are crowded with messages from Mantiev. On 2/2505.01 two Klingon battle groups entered the Mantiev system. They attacked and destroyed the Mantiev's Mobile Defense Force and the garrison in the Mantiev system. After the battle, the Klingons boarded the repair outpost in orbit around the planet. They took the outpost in tow and left the system.
2/2505.03 • Mantiev reports that the Klingons have left their system. The Klingon forces appeared to split up.
A group of ten medium Klingon ships headed "northwest".
A group of three large, six medium, and three small Klingon ships headed "north".
A group of three large, eighteen medium, and four small Klingon ships headed "south".
A group of two medium Klingon ships headed "west". This is the group with the captured repair facility.
2/2505.05 • The Landis Wavamm system comes on the communications waves reporting that it has thrown off the Klingon garrison. When the garrison's squadron of star ships left the system, the inhabitants were able to defeat the Klingon forces. They urgently request supplies of small arms and warships to allow themselves to keep their independence.
2/2505.06 • A moderate level protest is received at sector headquarters from the headquarters of the AOFW Space Navy, noting that a number of Federation warships have recently entered AOFW space without prior warning or permission. This is not to be repeated.
2/2505.08 • The Landis Wavamm system reports that the garrison ships have returned. They are Roloc's Escort Squadron of three K-24A Winner class escorts. Will someone please come and drive them off?
2/2505.09 • Star Fleet headquarters has forwarded the important parts of a message received by the Federation foreign relations council from the government of the Mantiev Colonial Associaton. The recent attack on the Mantiev system has left them virtually defenseless. The largest surviving ship in their fleet is a Class VI repair tender, and the largest warship is a Class IV escort. They are pleading to have some warships sent to them. Star Fleet headquarters indicates that it has ordered two Fenlon class monitors be towed to Mantiev along with two Epsilon class cutters and an old Larson class destroyer taken from the reserve fleet. However, due to the long transit time for some of these vessels, Commander Triangle Sector is requested to provide whatever assistance he can to the MCA. In fulfilling this request Commander Triangle Sector is reminded that only ships with older technology should be transferred to the MCA.
• Sector intelligence has found out that the Altharra system has let it be commonly known that a Federation fleet under the command of Rear Admiral T'nek was at that system from 2/2505.02 through 2/2505.05.
