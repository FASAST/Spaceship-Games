Star Clash - United Federation of Planets Report
Stardates 2/2505.11 - 2/2505.20 (Days 41-50)

General Communications
2/2505.20 • From: Star Fleet Central Command
To: Commander Triangle Sector
   On 2/2504.29 and 2/2505.10 this headquarters ordered Vice Admiral Kinneson to report on his war plans. Report as ordered, immediately.
 
Task Force 24
2/2505.11 • The Task Force is at Bakers World in the AOFW. Repair work is complete, but the replacement crews have not arrived.
2/2505.12 • The assault ship Asia arrives with the replacement crews.
2/2504.13 • The Task Force departs for Farkin. At warp 6 it will arrive on 2/2505.17.
2/2505.17 • The Task Force arrives at Farkin. It detects five medium Klingon ships in the system. The planetary government notes the arrival and is happy because of the danger from the Klingons. The force also notes that there are no other Star Fleet ships present, contrary to expectations.
2/2505.18 • The Task Force departs for Flood. At warp 6 it will arrive on 2/2505.20. The system government is not happy, as the Klingon force is still at the edge of the system.
2/2505.20 • The Task Force arrives at Flood. It finds Task Force 65 there and Task Unit 24-2 rejoins the fleet.
 
Task Force 65
2/2505.11 • Task Force 65, along with Task Unit 24-2, is attempting to pacify the Farkin system.
2/2505.15 • The ground troop commander reports that all opposition has ceased. The planet is free of its Klingon garrison.
2/2505.16 • Rear Admiral Parksley informs the provisional government of Farkin that it is on its own as he takes his fleet off to Flood. At warp 6 it will arrive on 2/2505.18.
2/2505.18 • The Task Force arrives at Flood. It detects three medium sized Klingon ships in the system.
2/2505.19 • The Task Force lands troops.
2/2505.20 • Task Force 24 finally arrives in the system. Vice Admiral Kinneson orders Task Unit 24-2 to rejoin Task Force 24.
 
Task Force 89
2/2505.11 • Admiral T'nek attempts to taunt the Klingon force in the system into attacking the IKS force nearby. The Klingons are apparently amazed that a Vulcan would even try to taunt them. Their laughter prevents them from replying or taking action.
Failing to get the watching forces to engage, T'nek orders the two battle cruisers to go after the IKS ships. The IKS ships retreat at warp speed to the "south".
2/2505.12 • The battle cruisers charge out against the Klingon force. It quickly withdraws from the system.
2/2505.13 • The Klingon garrison force returns to the system.
2/2505.18 • Three medium sized Klingon warp signatures approach the system from the "southwest".
2/2505.19 • The ground force commander reports that more time will be needed to completely eliminate the Klingon resistance on the planet.
 
Other Units
2/2505.13 • Landis Wavamm reports that three medium sized Klingon ships have come into the system.
2/2505.14 • Landis Wavamm is calling for help. The Klingon ships which just entered the system are raiding the planet. The other Klingon ships are sitting away from the planet and doing nothing.

• Laflin sends a message that a large Klingon force passed the planet on 2/2505.17 headed "north".
• Nicolii sends a message that a large Klingon force passed the planet on 2/2505.17 headed "northeast".
2/2505.19 • Derigo sends a message that a large Klingon force passed the planet on 2/2505.18 headed "north".
 
Common
2/2505.11 • Detachment 1 (Aragor and Singles) is approached by a Klingon W-2 Koreba class warpshuttle while on patrol. The destroyers ignore the Klingon. It falls back and rejoins the other small Klingon ship watching the destroyers. As the Detachment heads "west" the Klingons fall behind and eventually disappear from the sensors.
• The 14th and 15th Destroyer Divisions at Turbulence are ordered to join Task Force 89 at New Paradise. This is designated as Detachment 2. It leaves for Wall on its way south. At warp 6 it will arrive on 2/2505.14.
• The Akagi at Turbulence is ordered to join Task Force 24 at Farkin. It leaves for Wall on its way south. At warp 6 it will arrive on 2/2505.14.
• The 53rd Destroyer Division at Star Base 10 is ordered to Iovine. At warp 7 it will arrive on 2/2505.13.
• The cruiser Boeing at Derigo is to be turned over to the MCA. It disembarks 80% of its crew (260) on the planet. It is to go to Iovine to pick up other ships. This is designated Detachment 6. At warp 10 it will arrive on 2/2505.12.
2/2505.12 • The destroyers Williams and Tennant at Iovine disembark 80% of their crews (284) and join Detachment 6. The Detachment heads for Mantiev. To avoid detection it must take a circuitous route via Laflin, Lanroche, Hadalib, Rockhaven, Takanarra, and Mantiev. At warp 7 it will arrive on 2/2505.24.
• The Asia (Detachment 3) arrives at Baker's World. The personnel onboard are transferred to the ships in Task Force 24 to bring them up to full strength.
• The Akagi passes through Wall on its way south toward Farkin. At warp 8 it will arrive on 2/2505.16.
2/2505.13 • The Asia (Detachment 3) departs Baker's World headed for Iovine. At warp 7 it will arrive on 2/2505.15.
• The 53rd Destroyer Division arrives at Iovine. It takes up duty as the garrison.
• The Colil Squadron reports that two small Klingon warp signatures approach the Colil system from the "northwest". They hover for a couple of hours and then depart back to the "northwest".
2/2505.14 • The 14th and 15th Destroyer Divisions (Detachment 2) arrives at Wall on its way to New Paradise. At warp 6 it will arrive on 2/2505.26.
2/2505.15 • The Asia (Detachment 3) arrives at Iovine. It picks up 284 crew from the Boeing.
2/2505.16 • The Asia (Detachment 3) departs Iovine for Derigo. At warp 7 it will arrive on 2/2505.17.
• The Akagi arrives at Farkin. Task Force 24 is not there, so it joins Task Unit 24-2 as part of Task Force 65.
2/2505.17 • The Asia (Detachment 3) arrives at Derigo. It picks up 260 crew from the Williams and Tennant.
2/2505.18 • The Asia (Detachment 3) departs Derigo for Star Base 10. At warp 7 it will arrive on 2/2505.19.
2/2505.19 • A huge Klingon force (73 ships) arrives at Star Base 10. In a short engagement they destroy the combat ships in the garrison and the two outposts. They suffer only minor damage.
The Asia (Detachment 3) arrives at Star Base 10. Detecting a huge battle in progress, the ship turns and heads out of the sector at warp 7.
