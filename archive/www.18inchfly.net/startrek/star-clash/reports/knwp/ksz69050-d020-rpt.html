Battle Group Ker'Chuck
2/2504.11 • The group is on its way to the Farkin system.
2/2504.13 • The battle group arrives at the Farkin system and attacks the Federation fleet there in the Battle of Farkin. Unexpectedly, Ker'chuck is victorious! Despite losses, he routs the Federation fleet.
The Federation fleet withdraws at warp 6 to the "northeast".
Realizing that he would be unable to hold the system against the Star Fleet force in the Flood system, he takes his damaged ships in tow and heads for Starbase 1 via the Flood system. At warp 6, the battle group will arrive at Flood on 2/2504.15.
2/2504.15 • At the Flood system the battle group detects twenty-two Size B and three Size A Federation warp signatures. The Federation force responds by advancing.
Having observed the Federation force, Ker'chuck accepts that his injured fleet is no match for what is in the system. His orders are to ignore the enemy force and proceed home. The fleet departs the Flood system for Starbase 1 at warp 6 to arrive on 2/2504.20.
2/2504.20 • The battle group arrives at Starbase 1. The base reports that it is ready to begin repairs on the ships.
