Battle Group Krraa'Zi
2/2505.01 • Battle Group Krraa'zi arrives at the Mantiev system and joins with Battle Group Massavid and the two transports in Detachments 6 and 7. They detect seven Size B and eleven Size A Romulan signatures, plus four Size B and five Size A Klingon signatures. The combined battle groups immediately move to attack.
At the Massacre at Mantiev the combined Klingon force destroys all but one of the ships in the Mantiev system and captures the F-2 Nestar repair facility there. The Klingons take moderate damage to two cruisers and light damage to another.
2/2505.02 • Pulrok's Support Squadron is received into the fleet from Battle Group Massavid.
Admiral Kraa'zi transfers his standard to the cruiser Swooping Hawk.
All ships in the battle group except Vargus' Squadron, Krasher's Squadron, Bulgar's Support Squadron, and Pulrok's Support Squadron are detached and ordered to proceed together toward Federation Star Base 10. They are to schedule their movement so as to arrive on 2/2505.23. This is designated as "Detachment 1".
The reduced Battle Group Krraa'Zi heads for Starbase 1 at warp 7 for repairs. They expect to arrive on 2/2505.08.
2/2505.08 • The battle group Krraa'Zi arrives at Starbase 1. The outposts inform Admiral Krraa'Zi that they are ready to begin repairs on the next day.
2/2505.09 • The S-8B Murph repair facilities use the day to set up their gantries so they can repair the battle group's damage.
The fleet begins repairing. The best estimate is that repairs will be completed on 2/2505.13.
A total of 34 crew and 27 marines are transferred from the replacement pool on the outposts to bring the crews of the damaged ships up to full strength.

Common
2/2505.02 • The communications channels are crowded with messages from Mantiev. On 2/2505.01 two Klingon battle groups entered the Mantiev system. They attacked and destroyed the Mantiev's Mobile Defense Force and the garrison in the Mantiev system. After the battle, the Klingons boarded the repair outpost in orbit around the planet. They took the outpost in tow and left the system.
2/2505.03 • Mantiev reports that the Klingons have left their system. The Klingon forces appeared to split up.
A group of ten medium Klingon ships headed "northwest".
A group of three large, six medium, and three small Klingon ships headed "north".
A group of three large, eighteen medium, and four small Klingon ships headed "south".
A group of two medium Klingon ships headed "west". This is the group with the captured repair facility.
2/2505.05 • With the removal of the ships in the Klingon garrison the people of Landis Wavamm rise up in open rebellion. The sudden attack eliminates a notable portion of the troops on the planet.
2/2505.06 • The Altharra system makes it commonly known that a Federation fleet under the command of Rear Admiral T'nek was at that system from 2/2505.02 through 2/2505.05. It included three large and twenty six medium sized ships, one of which was the cruiser Lifeforce. The force left Altharra headed "southwest" at warp 6 followed by three Klingon ships.
2/2505.08 • The New Paradise garrison reports that a force composed of eight large, four medium, and two small Federation warp signatures has entered the system. M'cadi's Patrol Squadron withdraws in the face of overwhelming odds. The Star Fleet unit approaches the planet, and begins unloading troops. Soon afterwards New Paradise ceases to communications.
2/2505.09 • M'cadi's Patrol Squadron returns to New Paradise. The Federation fleet is still there. The unit takes up a position well away from Star Fleet force. It also notes that three medium Klingon warp signatures are present on the other side of the system.
