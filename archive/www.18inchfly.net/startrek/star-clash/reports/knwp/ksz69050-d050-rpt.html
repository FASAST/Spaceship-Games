Other Units
2/2505.11 • Detachment 1 (Kapul's Battle Squadron, Rotneb's Squadron, L'mac's Squadron, and Riven's Scout Squadron) is ordered to divert from its course to Federation Star Base 10 and to join with Admiral Krraa'zi at New New Aberdeen. It is to take a circuitous route to avoid as many systems in the AOFW as possible. At warp 6 it will arrive on 2/2505.19.
2/2505.12 • At New Paradise two large ships break off from the Federation fleet and race toward the watching Klingon garrison ships. The garrison again withdraws to the "west" at warp speed.
2/2505.13 • The garrison returns to New Paradise. The IKS ships do not seem to have returned as expected.
• At Landis Wavamm three medium Klingon warp signatures are detected approaching the system. The newcomers move in to attack. The garrison stands by the planet. However, the approaching IKS squadron commander (Captain Ribpanno) begins negotiations with the Klingon squadron commander (Captain Roloc). Roloc agrees to allow Ribpanno to plunder the planet as a punishment for its rebellion. Ribpanno is informed that a Klingon assault force is on its way, and things will change when they arrive. The IKS ships warily approach the planet and begin beaming troops down.
2/2505.16 • Detachment 4 (Power Shifter and Margok's Cruiser Squadron) arrives at Landis Wavamm. They join the three ships already there. As they approach, the three IKS ships beam up personnel and depart the system headed "northwest".
2/2505.17 • The force at Landis Wavamm lands 900 marines to begin re-conquering the planet. As soon as the troops have landed Detachment 4 heads back to Outpost 2.
 
Common

The Klingon commander ignores the message. A short while later two large ships break off from the Federation fleet and race toward the IKS ships. The IKS units withdraw to the "south" at warp speed.
