Battle Group Ker'Chuck
2/2504.01 • M'ragger's Squadron is attached to the battle group from Starbase 1.
The troop transport Death Hauler along with its 800 troops is attached to the battle group from Starbase 1.
The battle group heads for the Rockhaven system at Warp 6 to arrive in 5 days.
2/2504.06 • The battle group arrives at Rockhaven. There it detects two Size A Federation warp signatures which quickly depart the system; one headed northeast (towards the AOFW?), and one headed north. Communications from the planet are detected aimed towards the Federation, the AOFW, and the Romulan Empire. No resistance is encountered within the system. Troops are landed, and an occupation established.
2/2504.09 • Reacting to the news of a Federation force in the occupied Farkin system, the battle group reloads its troops and immediately heads for Farkin at Warp 6 to arrive in 3 days. Admiral Ker'Chuk intends to destroy the Federation force, or die trying. Before departing for Farkin, Admiral Ker'Chuk composed a message to the other Klingon commanders:
"Brother Klingons, I go now to do Glorious Battle with our enemies, the Federation. I need you to immediately leave the comfort of your pleasure planets and come to fight at my side to save the FARKIN system while we bathe ourselves in glory! If, like Federation women, you decide against committing to this battle, then I will send you my next communication from the halls of Stoval Kor, a place you will obviously never see! IT IS A GOOD DAY TO DIE. Ker'Chuck"
