Star Clash - Independents Report

This is the Independents Campaign Report section of the Star Clash campaign. Independents are the Affiliation of Outer Free Worlds (AOFW), Orion Frontier Mercantile Association (OFMA), Mantiev Colonial Association (MCA), and the various independent systems within The Triangle. Although the Imperial Klingon States (IKS) is officially an Independent, it has a controlling player and is treated as a player nation.
