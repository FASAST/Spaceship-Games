Star Clash - Independents Report
Days 2/2504.01 - 2/2504.10

Affiliation of Outer Free Worlds (AOFW)
2/2504.05 • A force of two Federation support ships arrives at the Colil system. They are given permission to wait there until other ships arrive.
2/2504.06 • Two large forces of Federation ships are detected by a listening post in the Archibald system heading "south". Over the course of the next few days the Fountainworld and Paxton systems also detect the forces. The Federation forces leave detection range on 2/2504.10
2/2504.07 • A Federation task force (a battle cruiser, four destroyers, and two scouts) arrives at the Colil system. The support ships join the unit which departs for the Flitner system.

Independent Worlds
2/2504.02 • Schneiter - A Star Fleet force arrives at the system. It is greeted by the system's garrison of two Greyhound warpshuttles. The leaders are happy to see the force, but disappointed that it will not remain to protect them from the Romulans. The force leaves the next day.
2/2504.04 • Gamon - A Star Fleet force arrives at the system. All but one of the system's garrison remain out of detailed detection range. While pleased to see a patrolling Star Fleet force, they are not anxious for it to stay, as they fear it could lead to Romulan interest in their system. They are pleased when the fleet announces its intentions to leave within a day. The force leaves the next day.
• Meadow - A Star Fleet force arrives at the system. Knowing their two small Romulan built destroyers are no match for the Federation ships, they keep them near to the inhabited planet. The system signals to the Romulan Empire for help. The Federation force leaves the next day. The planetary government sends an additional message to the Romulans asking for small arms and training advisors.
2/2504.05 • Colil - Star Fleet Task Unit 89-3 enters the system. When challenged by the Baird and the Atkinson (McDonald class frigates), it says it has classified communications for Star Fleet's Colil Squadron. The unit is allowed to enter the system, temporarily. The unit is also waiting for the arrival of another Star Fleet force.
2/2504.06 • Satterfeld - Task Unit 89-1 enters the system. It is met at the edge of the system by the system's home defense fleet. A gala ball is quickly arrange to celebrate the arrival. Everyone is extremely polite and obviously happy at the fleet's presence, but is disappointed to hear that it cannot stay. The force leaves the next day.
• Rockhaven - A Klingon fleet enters the system. They system's two warpshuttles head "north" to seek help from the AOFW and the Federation. The Klingons land 800 troops and begin efforts to pacify the planet.
2/2504.07 • Curie - A Star Fleet force approaches the system. It is met by two Larson class destroyers. The planet asks for reinforcements; which are politely denied by the fleet commander, Admiral T'nek, noting the strategic needs of the fleet. The force leaves the next day.
2/2504.08 • Colil - Task Unit 89-2 enters the system. The unit is allowed to enter the system, temporarily. Task Units 89-2 and 89-3 join and leave the next day.
2/2504.09 • Farkin - A Star Fleet force arrives at the system. As it approaches the three Klingon garrison ships flee to the "west".
• Rockhaven - The Klingon fleet suddenly embarks its troops and departs the system at warp 6 headed "south".
2/2504.10 • Flitner - A large Star Fleet force arrives at the system. The gunboat Ranmorson (Guardian class gunboat) meets them at the edge of the system and asks about their business. Rear Admiral T'nek, the commander, says they are scouting the area for Klingon or Romulan activity. Entry to the system is politely, but firmly denied. The force leaves the next day.
• Flood - A Star Fleet force arrives at the system. As it approaches the three Klingon garrison ships flee to the "west".
