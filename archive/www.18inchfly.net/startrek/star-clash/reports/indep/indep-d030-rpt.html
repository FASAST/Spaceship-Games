Days 2/2504.21 - 2/2504.30

Affiliation of Outer Free Worlds (AOFW)

2/2504.22 • Repair work begins at Baker's World on the Star Fleet ships in Task Force 24.
2/2504.25 • Intelligence reports indicate that Klingon forces are on the move in the southern part of The Triangle. This information is being shared with the Federation, OFMA, and MCA.

Orion Frontier Mercantile Association (OFMA)
2/2504.26 • An intelligence report from the AOFW indicates that Klingon forces are on the move in the southern part of The Triangle. That does not seem to be a threat to the OFMA.

Mantiev Colonial Association (MCA)
2/2504.26 • An intelligence report from the AOFW indicates that Klingon forces are on the move in the southern part of The Triangle. MCA forces are placed on a higher alert status.

2/2504.30 • The Mobile Defense Force arrives at Mantiev.

Gorn Alliance
2/2504.21 • Nothing of note happens.

Independent Worlds
2/2504.21 • Farkin - Two more Star Fleet ships arrive at the system from the "north". They join the fleet already there.
• Flood - Two Federation ships approach the system but turn to the "east".
2/2504.24 • Farkin - Two more Star Fleet ships arrive at the system from the "west". They join the fleet already there.
2/2504.25 • Farkin - The Star Fleet force in this system finally initiates an assault on the planet. 1,100 Federation marines land and begin efforts to subdue the Klingon occupation force.
2/2504.29 • Deerlam - Star Fleet's Task Force 89 completes its joint exercises and departs the system at warp 6 headed "southwest". It is followed by the three Klingon ships.
• Meadow - For the second time the system sends a formal request for assistance and defense against Federation incursions. They ask that a squadron of warships be sent to the system, and that advisors and small arms be provided to train and arm their security personnel. They are not happy that the Romulans have not replied to the previous request.
