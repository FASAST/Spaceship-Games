Star Clash

A Star Trek Campaign
using the Starship Tactical Combat Simulator

Star Clash is a relatively simple campaign set in FASA's version of the Star Trek universe beginning in the year 2/25. Its primary intent is to generate tactical battles and to get the players to spend a lot of time talking about what they plan to do.

This site supports the campaign by providing a place to access general information and allows the players to keep track of what is going on.

With the end of the campaign the Status Reports have been linked in through this page. The Reports for the last active week of the campaign (2/2505.11 - 2/2505.19, days 41 - 49) are present. The entries for the last week were taken directly from the rough notes. Some errors may be present, and not all cross referenced information is included.
