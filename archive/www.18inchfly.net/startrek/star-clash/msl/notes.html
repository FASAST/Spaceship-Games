Star Clash - Master Ship Listing
Notes on the Master Ship Listing

  Version 1.3.4
 
1)  This is not an all inclusive listing of Star Trek ships.
  Generally, only the ships available at the time of the start of the campaign (2/2504) or which may come into play are included.
  The major powers (Federation, Klingon, Romulan, Gorn) are limited to the ships listed in their sections.
  The minor powers (AOFW, IKS, etc.) are limited only by what they are able to acquire from other sources.
 
2)  Class Name
  Any ship class with the letters "NC" attached is the "Non-Cloak" version of the ship.
 
3)  Ref (Reference)
  Fed = Federation Ship Recognition Manual
  Kling = Klingon Ship Recognition Manual
  Rom = Romulan Ship Recognition Manual
  STCS = Starship Tactical Combat Simulator rules book
  DHT = David H. Ternes design
  BF = Brent Fisher design
  RG = Rob Gray design
  SD = Stardate design
 
4)  Avail (Availability)
  VC = Very Common
  C = Common
  UC = Uncommon
  R = Rare
  VR = Very Rare
  Availability is relative to the individual navy.
 
5)  Com (Commonness)
  2 = 2nd line equipment
  1 = 1st line equipment
  N = Newest equipment
  The older equipment is, the more likely a side is to receive it, if requested.
 
6)  Eng (Engines)
  F = Federation
  K = Klingon
  R = Romulans
  O = Orions
  G = Gorn
