Star Trek Campaigns
Game Counters

These are the source graphics for the Game counters used in the tactical game. Most of the graphics contain up to a page of hexagonal ship counters. Some graphics have support counters (planets, explosions, etc.), and one contains the "Fire / No Fire Chits". Counters have a black background with white ship images.

Using The Counter Pages

  • Download an image.
  • Print the image using the highest quality setting of your printer.
  • For backing material use a heavy paper, poster board, cardboard, or even a thin foam board.
    For an all-black counter, the backing material used should be black.
  • Use a strong spray adhesive to attach the printed image to the backing material.
  • Carefully cut out the counters.
