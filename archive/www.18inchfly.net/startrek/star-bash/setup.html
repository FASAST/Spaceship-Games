Star Bash - Campaign Setup

Here is what to do to join the campaign:

    • Live in or around Fort Wayne, Indiana.
    • Contact the Campaign Master and let me know you want to participate. Indicate which side (Federation or Klingon) you want to play.
    • Go to the Documents page. Check to see that you have the necessary software. Download the Star Bash Rules, Squadron Organizer, and Movement Calculator.
    • Use the Squadron Organizer to determine the ships in your squadron.
    • Determine the attributes of your commander (see the campaign rules).
    • Select a name for your commander. Be reasonable; no well known names (ex. "Kirk" is out).
    • Send all the information to the Campaign Master.
    • Wait.
