Star Bash - Campaign News

This page contains Campaign News - that is, anything of long term value which needs to be available to all the players.

    • Campaign Rules Modification - Under the Probes rule, Federation Reliant class cruisers will be treated as Research Vessels. This will be added to the next revision of the Campaign Rules (Version 1.5) whenever that is creted.
    • D-19B Viper's Tongue - The recently designed D-19B Viper's Tongue class of Klingon destroyers will not be available for selection as part of a starting Klingon squadron. Starting in 2/2606 the D-19B will be treated as a Unique design.
