Star Bash

A Star Trek Campaign
using the Starship Tactical Combat Simulator

Star Bash is a simple campaign set in FASA's version of the Star Trek universe beginning in the year 2/26. Its primary intent is to generate reasonalbe sized tactical battles.

This site supports the campaign by providing a place to access general information and allows the players to keep track of what is going on.
