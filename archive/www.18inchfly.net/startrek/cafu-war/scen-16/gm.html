Star Trek Campaigns - Brentanus-Cafu War
Scenario 16 - Rout At Windhaven

Game Master Notes

Scenario 16, prepared by David Ternes

The following information is for the use of the Game Master. Players should not have access to this page.

Map.

Across the center of the map is an asteroid belt. It is three to four asteroids thick, with three to four hexes between each asteriod.

Setup.

The Klingon fleets should start as far apart as possible on their side of the battle area. The Federation fleet should be about in the center of its side.

Victory Conditions.

This is a stand up fight. The side which destroys the most, without excessive losses wins.
