Klingon 12th War Fleet

Orders. The 12th War Fleet will proceed at its best speed to the vicinity of the Windhaven system. There it will seek out and destroy the Star Fleet force which should be near the system.

Game Notes.

  1. A player may command ships in only one battle fleet.
  2. Ships in the same squadron must be in the same hex in the initial formation. All squadrons in a battle fleet must be within 6 hexes of each other in their initial formation.
  3. The battle fleets must be a minimum of 30 hexes apart (nearest ship to nearest ship).
  4. Ship commanders within each battle fleet may communicate freely. Ships in different battle fleets may not communicate with each other or with the other battle fleet commander. Only the battle fleet commander may communicate with the other battle fleet commander. Units openly discussing tactics so that other units are able to overhear them will be penalized by the Game Master with the application of arbitrary damage to their ships.
