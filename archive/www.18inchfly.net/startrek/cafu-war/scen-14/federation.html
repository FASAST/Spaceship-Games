Scenario 14 - Chance Encounter At Revena IV

the United Federation of Planets

Scenario 14, prepared by David Ternes

Task Force 517

Task Force 517 has been ordered to patrol near the Revena system. While on this patrol they contacted a group of Klingon ships which included an unusually large vessel. The Task Force has moved to investigate.

Orders. Patrol the area around the Revena system. It is to deny the use of the area to all enemy forces.

Intelligence. A minor Klingon transit route passes through the edge of the patrol area, however this is not expected to create any notable problems.
