Scenario 05, prepared by David Ternes

Magnot's Patrol Group

The patrol group was created to perform general scouting and deep patrol duties in a quite zone of the sector. While checking the Drake system, the group encounters a Federation force which appears to be transiting the system. Merriment ensues.

Orders. The patrol group will scout its assigned area. It may engage and destroy any force which the commander feels the force will be able to defeat. Any losses must be notably less than proportional to those of the enemy.

Intelligence. There is no enemy activity known or expected in this area.
