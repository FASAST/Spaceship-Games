Brentanus-Cafu War

A Star Trek Campaign
using the Starship Tactical Combat Simulator

The Brentanus-Cafu War was a campaign started by Brent Fisher sometime back about 1993 to 1994 and run by Brent Fisher and David Ternes. Its primary purpose was to provide a background setting for combat scenarios to be fought using the Starship Tactical Combat Simulator.

The scenarios are listed as close to the original order of play as can be reconstructed. Scenarios with letter Ids were created for the early part of the campaign, but not recorded at the creation time, and so some were lost to history. Id numbers were assigned in the order later scenarios were written, not as actually played.
