Scenario 09, prepared by David Ternes

Federation Task Force 364

Task Force 364 has been patrolling the area around the Renseigne system looking for a Klingon battle force reported to be in the area. A number of smaller groups of Klingon ships were detected, but all proved to be too far away and too skittish to catch.

Orders. Task Force 364 is to find and destroy any enemy forces in or near the Renseigne system.

Intelligence. The exact size of the expected enemy force is not known.

Force. The task force includes the following units and vessels.

Game Notes.

  1. The Federation task force commander must prepare a formation diagram prior to setup. The lead ship of the force may start anywhere up to 20 hexes in from the edge of the board.
  2. The destroyers must be within 4 hexes of each other. All ships in the task force must be within 8 hexes of the flagship of the task force.
