Pradro's Raiding Group

A small group of Klingon raiders, known as Pradro's Raiding Group, has been organized to cruise the Federation supply lines looking for easy pickings. While scouting the H't Bui Bet system, the Raiding Froup detected the approach of three Star Fleet warships. When it became clear that the warships would enter the system, Pradro decided to attempt an ambush.
