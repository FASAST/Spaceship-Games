Star Trek Campaigns - Brentanus-Cafu War
Scenario K - Second Engagement At H't Bui Bet

Game Master Notes

Scenario K, prepared by David Ternes

Victory Conditions

This is a stand up battle with no special objectives other than the destruction of the enemy. That said, if the Federation looses its battleship, it looses the fight.

Setup

There is a single planet on the setup chart. The Klingons may place their ships anywhere in their half of the battle area (i.e., the half away from the Federation entry side).
