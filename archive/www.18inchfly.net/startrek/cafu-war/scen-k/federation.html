Scenario K, prepared by David Ternes

Federation Task Force 127

A reconstituted Task Force 127 was sent back to the H't Bui Bet system. Sensors detected enemy ships within the system. The commander of the force decided that they should investigate.

Orders. Find the enemy. Destroyer them.

Intelligence. Sensors detected six Klingon ships. As the Federation force entered the system the ships disappeared from the sensors, almost certainly due to cloaking.

Force. Task Force 127 includes the following vessels.

Game Notes.
