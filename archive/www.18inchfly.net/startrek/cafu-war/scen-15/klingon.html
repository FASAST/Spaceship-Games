Atkiner's Battle Group

During the latest push, a battle group sent to take the Raisson system was intercepted by a Star Fleet task force. Unfortunately the Federation force encountered was too strong for the battle group, and it withdrew. This battle group has been formed to patrol the Raisson system and deny its use to light Federation forces.

Orders. Patrol the Raisson system. Prevent the system's use by any enemy forces not clearly superior to the battle group.

Intelligence. The enemy's intentions are not known, however they have shown interest in this system in the recent past.
