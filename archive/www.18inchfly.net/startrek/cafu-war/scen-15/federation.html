the United Federation of Planets

Scenario 15, prepared by David Ternes

Federation Task Force 521

During the Klingon's latest push, a scratch task force of Star Fleet ships was sent to hold the Raisson system. Fortunately the Klingon force encountered quickly withdrew when it saw the strength of the ships in the Federation force. Task Force 521 is now being sent to the Raisson system to insure that the Klingons do not use the system until Star Fleet is ready to counterattack.

Orders. Task Force 521 is to patrol the Raisson system and deny the use of the area to all enemy forces.

Intelligence. Nothing is known about Klingon intentions in this area, however, they sent a modest force to the system in the past. So, be prepared!
