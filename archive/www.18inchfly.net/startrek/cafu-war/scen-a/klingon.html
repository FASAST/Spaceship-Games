Scenario A, prepared by David Ternes

Farkar's Squadron

The war with the Federation has just started. Commander Farkar has taken his squadron to look at a number of supposedly unimportant systems. He figures that there should be something worthwhile at one of them.

Intelligence. As the squadron passes the Cando system it detects some Federation ships. Three appear to be warships and two are freighters. This looks like the "something" for which Farkar was looking.

Orders. Destroy the Federation warships and capture the freighters.
