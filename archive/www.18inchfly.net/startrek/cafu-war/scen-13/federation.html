Federation Convoy KF341

Convoy KF341 is a typical frontier convoy. It is composed of a small group of merchant ships, a few Star Fleet auxiliary freighters, and an escort of warships. It has just completed the transfer of cargo at New Galena. As it prepares to move away from the planet, a Klingon raiding group appears.

Orders. The escort for convoy KF341 is to protect the convoy from the enemy raiding force.

Game Notes.

  1. The Federation commander must assign the ships of the convoy according to the Federation setup chart. Ships must be placed as allocated on that chart. For the purposes of set up the USS Troll and the USS Orc are considered to be warships.
  2. None of the merchant ships are armed (regardless of what their Control Panels may indicate). Merchant ships may not scan or lock on to an enemy ship.
  3. All freighters are considered to be loaded with miscellaneous cargoes except the USS Troll which is empty.
