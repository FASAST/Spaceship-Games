the United Federation of Planets

Scenario D, prepared by David Ternes

Federation Task Force 127

Task Force 127 was passing the H't Bui Bet system when sensors detected enemy ships within the system. The commander of the force decided that they should investigate.

Orders. Find the enemy. Destroyer them.

Intelligence. Sensors detected five ships, three Klingon and two Romulan. As the Federation force entered the system the ships disappeared from the sensors, almost certainly due to cloaking.

Force. Task Force 127 includes the following vessels.

      USS Yamato (NCC-1705) Enterprise Mark III Cruiser XI 75
      USS Maelstrom (NCC-8600) Andor Mark IV Cruiser XI 78

Game Notes.

  1. The Federation player will set up on the board second, after the Klingon and Romulan players have prepared their dispositions.
  2. Federation ships may setup anywhere along the lower map sheet edge (lower from the Federation's point of view).
