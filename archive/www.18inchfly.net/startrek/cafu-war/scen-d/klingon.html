Margrud's Squadron

Although allied, the Klingons and Romulans have never been the best of friends. In an attempt to improve cooperation between the fleets, a number of mixed units have been formed. Margrud's Destroyer Squadron is one of these. Two Romulan cruisers have been temporarily attached to the squadron to allow each nation to observe the other's battle methods.

Orders. Margrud's Squadron went to the H't Bui Bet system looking for some easy prey along the Federation supply routes. Soon after they entered the system, their sensors detected three Star Fleet warships approaching. When the Star Fleet ships turned toward H't Bui Bet, Margrud decided to set an ambush.
