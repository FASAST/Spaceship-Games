Star Trek Races

I have created racial modifiers for nearly every Star Trek race in the Alpha and Beta Quadrants. I understand why the creators of the FASA system restricted players, but Star Trek Universe has become so rich that opening up the number of races people can play seemed essential.

Game Masters must work with their players to guard against power gaming. Plus, while freeing, this many races can lead to choice paralysis. However, I feel that vast number possibilities will provide a wealth of character development and spur players to more creative than ever when creating characters.

To learn more about the races and to discover whether or not hybrids are possible, please visit our friends over at Memory Alpha.
