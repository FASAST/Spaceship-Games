UNCLASSIFIED EXTRACT - NCC-3347 MISSION LOGS - STARDATE 9977.14 - .16 INCLUSIVE

 

 

Captain’s Log, stardate 9977.14, Captain Danislav in command of USS Dakota on routine patrol of the Neutral Zone. We have received an emergency call from Federation Deep Space Outpost A-97, which may be under attack by Romulan forces. Luckily we are nearby and will be able to rendezvous with the USS Lexington in Quadrant 411 in a matter of minutes.

 

Captain’s Log, stardate 9977.15. Have arrived in system with Lexington.  Initial sensor sweeps indicate a Romulan D’Daridex-class Battlecruiser in system approaching the planet our outpost orbits. As per previous arrangement with the captain of the Lexington we will move immediately to support the outpost.

 

USS Dakota NCC-3347 initiating Red Alert, repeat Red Alert. The D’Daridex-class Battlecruiser, now positively identified as the Romulus RL-D13, has just opened fire at long range with plasma weapons.
