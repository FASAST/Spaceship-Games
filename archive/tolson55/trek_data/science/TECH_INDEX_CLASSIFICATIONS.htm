/ THE SOCIALPOLITICAL INDEX

Social Science Index Classifications

The social science index, like the hard sciences indices within the technological index, is a linear progression in which 0 indicates no achievements and 9 indicates the greatest achievement known to the Federation at the time the index was determined. The table below gives the divisions of index examples for each division.

    Rating Accomplishment

    1. 0    No accomplishment

    2. 1    Recognition of formal leadership
    3. 2    Development of religion; specialization in professions
    4. 3    Development of social classes; symbolic economics
    5. 4    Basic socioeconomic theory
    6. 5    Basic psychology of own race
    7. 6    Psychoanalysis; behavior modification
    8. 7    Large-scale social planning
    9. 8    Elimination of racial, cultural, or sexual prejudice
    10. 9    Psychological theories and principles about alien races

 

Cultural Attitude Index Classification

The cultural attitude is more circular than linear, with no division of the index considered inherently superior. The progression to 9 continues back to 0. Thus, 0 does not indicate no accomplishment as in the other indices. This is not to say that cultures need necessarily move along the chart in one direction or another. Federation members and associated cultures display a wide variety of index ratings, from anarchy to monarchy to unity and back again. Many of these societies have developed from one classification or another, as indicated by the table, but a number of others have not. Terra (Earth) in the 20th century and Star Trek's time has a culture attitude index of 7. Vulcan has an 8, but just barely.
