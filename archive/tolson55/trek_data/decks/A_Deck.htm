A Deck – is composed of the Main Bridge and the VIP Docking Bay (Docking Port 1). The Main Bridge is the nexus for all command / control and communications activities of the ship. It is situated at the head of the computer core. Within its superstructure are the forward and lateral navigational sensor arrays the upper navigational sensor array, and two emergency battery banks (cold Fusion).
