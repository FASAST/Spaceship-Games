Camouflage Cover

Chameleon suit- This suit bends the light around the wearer essentially cloaking him from view with a fringe effect (think of the movie Predator). It has a 6 hour duration when on, having 360 power points, using 1 point of power per minute. Sensors and scanners can easily pick up the power field generated by this suit so it would trigger those kinds of alarms. It is used solely against sight and visual aided devices reducing a persons perception of the person by 60% when moving and 75% when stationary. These percentages are double over distance esentially making them totally invisible Modifiers from the weapon chart can apply to the persons (Intelligence characteristic) perception to make them chameleon harder to percieve. Of course if you are next to someone with the suit activated you could tell someone is there but not who.

Camouflage Netting- Simple netting coverage versus sight and visual aids. The netting does not leave any relavent signatures for sensing equipment and helps to hide profiles and obscure objects to any distant observer. Anyone up close can see the netting for what it is and may still have a difficult time being able to identify the objects underneath unless there are openings. Netting will reduce distant perception by 50% and close perception by 25%.

Camouflage Suit- Used by personnell to make it harder for observers to spot them. Over a distance it gives a 25% perception reduction.

Camouflage Masking Belts-  These belts are designed to fool sensor scans virtually making him invisible to those devices. The wearer must turn off any devices with active power / signal sources.   The only draw back is that an individual cannot move too fast, like running or a brisk paced walk. The reason being, the device has to take a snapshot of each area to mask the signature of the wearer. So it is essential for the wearer to walk to remain masked. These devices are usually used by Star Fleet Intelligence or elite units. These devices have a 8 hour duration.

Camouflage Holographic Projectors- These devices surround the area with a hologram essentially masking the area 100%. The projectors are  large and bulky and must be stationary when in use. They have an internal power source that can last from 2 - 8 hours depending on how big the projection area is. These devices are also able to use outside power sources. Sensors scans can detect the power source and energy emmision of these devices. The projection can be spotted if a person walks into the hologram or if there are some noticable flaws. Anyone up close will have a 10% chance of noticing a flaw or a 1 on a D10.
