Watchdogs- are computer-controlled emplacements that fire the equivalent of 2 modern rifle types at targets within their range. They track and lock onto pre-designated or non-designated life forms and or objects. The have 200 armor protection. Generators or their back up power cells can either power them. They have a Dex of 60 and Skill of 50 (this can be adjusted depending on the programmers and or engineers skill). These stationary automated sentries if active can lock onto targets using either a visual mode, and or scanning mode. The visual mode does not send out any detectable scanning emissions while active scanning does have detectible emmisions. These weapons are capable of aiming at targets and are usually set up behind partial cover for more protection.
