Heavy Weapons:

Heavy Weapons Specialist (Modern)- This skill is required to operate and maintain any of the weapons below, otherwise use of the weapon will be at 1/2 the DEX rating and double the action points required. A character should have a minimum STR of 50 to handle the weapons otherwise for each point under 50 is the penalty added to the use.

Scatter Phaser- It functions similar to a phaser rifle but with the advantage of a variable wide beam operation. For settings 2-6 the maximum range is 30 meters and requires 1 action point to set. The weapon fires in a fan shape, not a cone. It gives a +40 bonus to hit in the center area a +20 bonus near the edges. It has several spread settings. The weapon has 800 charges.

Setting 1 acts as a standard phaser rifle.
Setting 2 has an extended 6-meter spread at 2x the charge.
Setting 3 has an extended 9-meter spread at 4x the charge.
Setting 4 has an extended 12-meter spread at 6x the charge.
Setting 5 has an extended 15-meter spread at 8x the charge.
Setting 6 has an extended 18-meter spread at 10x the charge.
 
 

Repeater Phaser- it is a heavy phaser machine gun type weapon that has the range of a phaser rifle. It has two firing modes short burst or 5 shots up to a 45 degree angle (using 2 action points) or long burst which is up to 10 shots that can cover up to a 90 degree angle (using 3 action points) or a back and forth sweep in a 45 degree angle. It is a large bulky weapon and holds 1200 charges. This weapon does not have a wide beam option. If used against a single target roll once, there is a +20 bonus to hit the target for short burst and a +30 with a long burst. If the roll succeeds and is less than half that which is needed the damage is doubled. This weapon cannot use the aiming option upon firing at multiple targets. Also if firing near friendly targets (friendlies and enemy together) the weapons user must make a successful to hit roll to miss the friendly target with a -10 penalty. If the roll fails the friendly is hit.

Grenade Launcher- can hold up to 8 grenades and lob them up to 800 meters. It is also capable of adjusting phaser grenade settings.
