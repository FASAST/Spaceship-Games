Chart used for role-play damage applied to the superstructure.

Adapted from the program The Starship Creator by Simon And Schuster for FASA's RPG by T. Olson

This chart can also be used to show what systems maybe damaged, according to the hit location, from the damage chart. Just go to the component area that correspond to the hit location and roll.
