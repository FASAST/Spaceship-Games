FASA Optional Starship Combat Rules

These optional rules are designed to be used with FASA's Star Trek the Role Playing Game and Starship Combat Simulator. These optional rules work with most FASA materials including the Starship Construction Manual. Some of these rules are just enhancements of additional rules found in scenario books or are slightly similar to those found in other game systems. Other rules I made up to fill in a gap or hole in the game system that I, personally, found aggravating. Just a note, I have not play tested all of these rules, but the ones I have seem to work well. I just hope that these optional rules make the game more enjoyable, allowing role players more options with their ships or war gamers a new challenge of play.
Sincerely, T. Olson
