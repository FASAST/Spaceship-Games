Mortars- are indirect fire weapon that arc in on the target. A target can be within a line of sight or a forward observer can guide you in on the target. A forward observer with a tricorder actively engaged tied into a battle computer system will increase a To-Hit bonus by 10. It takes four-six phases to fire off a round depending on skill and conditions.

Photon Mortar’s- Have a 30 meter radius and do 100/70 damage with the ability to half armor ratings up to and not exceeding damage total. They also double damage to shields.

Sonic Mortar-will cause 80/60 damage within a 30 meter radius of impact. Can be set to stun or kill.
