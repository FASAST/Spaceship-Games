Phaser Cannon- The ship is equipped with two phaser cannon platforms that are in storage disassembled. When a need arises they can be assembled on ship and transported down by cargo transporters or carried down disassembled by shuttlecraft. The cannons are hover fire platforms that have a combination of gravity and magnetic cohesion locking plates for stability upon firing. They are powered by microwave emitters from the ship or by large bulky generators and have the equivalent firepower of a starship phaser at strength 3. The cannons microwave receptors can be powered up to 3 points and would get a +2 bonus for close range attacks and +1 for further ranged attacks. If firing through an atmosphere at targets in space or in orbit there would be no such bonus. A point of damage is the equivalent of 2000 points of hand weapon damage.

The phaser cannon is operated by one individual that has the skill phaser artillery or starship phasers (at a –20 penalty). The person would be seated in a chair attached to the left side of the cannon with hard cover and protection (200 armor) or this can be detached and used in a nearby location for remote operation allowing greater protection for the operator. He would have a sensor screen HUD display in front of him and controls in the arms. He can target either by direct fire or sensor/battle computer information transmitted to him from either ship or personnel with tricorders (acting as forward observers). Phaser cannon’s impact area can cover a 25-meter radius at the cost of 1point of power giving a +40 to hit modifier. This is a line of sight weapon with no arcing capability and takes 4 phases to fire a shot 6-8 if aquiring new targets by scanning. The weapon takes 3 turns to set up to fire if not already stationary (more or less moving the weapon into a different position and setting up to fire).

Disrupter Cannon- use the same rules for phaser cannons except that they do not lose the damage bonus for firing through an atmosphere.
