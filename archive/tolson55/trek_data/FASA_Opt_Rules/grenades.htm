Diffusion Grenades- will effectively reduce the To-Hit (Modern) rating of anyone firing either phasers or other charged particle weapons through a diffusion cloud by 20. Diffusion grenade clouds do not affect disrupter weapons. The effectiveness of a diffusion cloud extends to 30 meters and lasts for five minutes.

Sonic Grenade- will cause 50/20 points of damage to anyone within 20 meters of their impact. They can be set on stun or kill.

Phaser Grenade’s- have a 15 meter radius and a variety of settings with variable duration depending on setting. Can be set on a time delay or have a remote trigger. Total power in grenade is 16. Grazes still apply according to the to-hit number of the grenade along with modifiers for cover.
