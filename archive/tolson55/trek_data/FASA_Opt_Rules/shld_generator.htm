Portable Shield Generator- can be set to function at one of three operation ranges. Up to a 50-meter diameter, the shield can absorb up to 800 damage (all types). At a 100-meter diameter setting the shield can absorb up to 600 damage. At a 150-meter setting the shield can absorb up to 400 damage. The shield generator cannot be recharged once the capacitors have absorbed maximum damage levels, but can be re-energized by microwave power relay from an orbiting ship when not otherwise in use. Recharging takes one hour to perform. The shield generator cannot be used once the recharging cycle is activated.

Large Shield Generators- These are the equivalent to starship shields and require either microwave emitters to power them or a large power generators. 1 point of shielding is capable of absorbing an equivalent to 2000 points of hand weapon damage. The amount that this shield is able to absorb varies according to the shield available. Use the example for portable generators for power adjustment factors.
