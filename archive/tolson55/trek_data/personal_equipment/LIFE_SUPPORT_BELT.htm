LIFE SUPPORT BELT

 This wide, thick belt provides its wearer with air, temperature and heat regulation for up to 4 hours. It generates a glowing greenish-yellow force field around the wearer, and may be used like the more bulky environmental suit in space, in hostile environments, and underwater. Its main drawback, aside from the fact that its glow makes it impossible to hide while wearing one, is its vulnerability. A major impact can cause the belt to malfunction and fail and thus it is not used in combat situations. At the time of the five-year mission of the USS Enterprise under Captain Kirk, the belt is somewhat experimental and is only in use by Federation personnel on board larger ships, such as the Enterprise.
