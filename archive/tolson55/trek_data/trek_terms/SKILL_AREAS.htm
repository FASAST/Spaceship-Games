SKILL AREAS

Some skills may require that the character develope a separate Skill Rating in a specific division. These skills are preceded by a solid star (*). Examples are Medical Sciences, which requires a different Skill Rating for each racial type (Humans, Vulcans, etc.), and Armed Personal Combat, which requires a different rating for each weapon type. Sometimes a skill in one division may confer a lesser Skill Rating in another automatically.
