Starship Sensors

This skill involves operating a starship’s sensors probes to gather data for interpretation and storage in the ship’s computers. Development includes extensive training in the efficient use of the sensor controls and, when combined with other science skill, in the swift interpretation of the data gathered. All Star Fleet officers are trained in this area, but Navigators and Helmsmen receive advanced training and Science Officers receive professional-level training.

This skill is used to detect life and energy sources in other ships and on planets. The ship’s sensors also provide planetary gravity and climate data from standard orbit. All of this data may be interpreted by the Science Officer to give information to a prospective landing party. The skill also may be used by a Navigator to detect at long range moving objects likely to pass near a ship. In starship combat, the skill is used to provide data to the bridge crew about the enemy vessel, its preparations, its power allocation, and its damage.
