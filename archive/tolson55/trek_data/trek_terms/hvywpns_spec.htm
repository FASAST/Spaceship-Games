Heavy Weapons Specialist (Modern)-

This skill is required to operate and maintain any of the weapons below, otherwise use of the weapon will be at 1/2 the DEX rating and double the action points required. A character should have a minimum STR of 50 to handle the weapon otherwise for each point under 50 the difference is the penalty added to the use.

Scatter Phaser

Repeater Phaser

Grenade Launcher

Portable Cannon
