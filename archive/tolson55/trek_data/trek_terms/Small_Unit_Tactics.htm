Small Unit Tactics

This skill involves study of military and/or police tactics used in small skirmishes or commando actions. Training not only includes study of appropriate tactics, but also extensive guided practice in wargaming simulations not only with scale mockups but also in full-size field actions. Security Officers receive advanced training in this skill, and many have professional-level skill, particularly those who accompany or lead planetside scouting parties.

The skill would be used by a character desiring to set

up effective defenses for a landing party in a hostile area or to command a boarding party.
