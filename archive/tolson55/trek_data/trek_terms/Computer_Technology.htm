Computer Technology

This skill deals with the practical technical side of computers. Training includes some instruction in computer theory and guided practice in computer construction. Computer repair is stressed, and Engineering Officers, Science Officers, and Communications Officers have at least minimum qualification in this skill so that they can do routine maintenance on even the sophisticated computers aboard starships. Computer design and more complex computer theory are covered by the Physical Science skill of computer science.

This skill is used in the game for more-than-routine repairs. Such repairs are dealt with during training, but the more skilled an officer is in this field, the more successful he will be at difficult repairs.
