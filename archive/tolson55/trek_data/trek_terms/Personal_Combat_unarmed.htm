Personal Combat, Unarmed

This skill is all-inclusive; simulating all unarmed combat styles (judo, karate, boxing, etc.). Training involves physical conditioning, as well as instruction and guided practice in attack and defense modes, falls, special series of attacks, and so on. Though not all forms of unarmed combat are alike, separate Skill Ratings are not required in the separate forms.

The skill is used to help determine the To-Hit Number and the damage in hand-to-hand combat.
