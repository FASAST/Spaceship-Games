Transporter Operational Procedures

This skill involves the use of transporter devices, whether they be personnel transporters or cargo transporters. Training includes locking in on a person or an area, powering up the system, and accomplishing beamup with simulators and with all three transporter types commonly in use. All Star Fleet officers are trained in this skill.

This skill may be use when a character desires to make a quick lock-on and beam-up, when atmospheric conditions or other hazards make beaming difficult, or when extreme precision is required, such as transporting into an unknown area using sensor readings only.
