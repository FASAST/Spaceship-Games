Deflector Shield Technology

This skill involves the repair and maintenance of the devices that produce a starship’s protective screens and its tractor/pressor beams. Training involves instruction on the theory behind the devices as well as extensive guided practice constructing and repairing the devices and their controlling panels. Engineering Officers and Navigators are competent in this skill so that they may make emergency repairs to the equipment, even during starship combat.

The Navigator uses this skill in starship combat in case a bridge hit damages the Deflector Shield Panel. It also could be used if a character desired to construct a tractor/ pressor beam or shield generator while on an adventure.
